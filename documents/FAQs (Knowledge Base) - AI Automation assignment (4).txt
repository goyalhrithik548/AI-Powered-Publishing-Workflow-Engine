📤 What Happens After I Submit My Final Draft?
Once you submit your final manuscript through the dashboard, your book moves into the production queue. From this point onward, the steps differ slightly based on whether or not you’ve opted for any add-on services.
Let’s break it down:
________________


📝 If You Have Not Opted for Any Add-On Services
(This is referred to as the Limited Publishing route)
Your book will be published in the following formats:
            * 📘 Paperback – available on the BookLeaf Publishing Store

            * 📱 eBook – available on the BookLeaf Publishing Store

The standard publishing timeline is:
               * ⏳ 30 to 45 business days from the date of your final submission

During this period:
                  * Your book’s status in the dashboard will appear as “In Review”

                  * Our editorial and production team will work on:

                     * File formatting and validation

                     * ISBN assignment

                     * Cover alignment and integration

                     * Preparing distribution assets

                        * Once the publishing process is completed, the status will change to “Published”

📩 At that point, you will receive:
                           * An email with the purchase links for your book

                           * The same links will also be available on your author dashboard

You do not need to follow up during this phase. Rest assured, our team ensures everything is progressing smoothly in the background.
________________


🚀 If You Have Opted for the Bestseller Breakthrough Package
With this premium add-on, the entire publication process is fast-tracked.
The publishing timeline in this case is:
                              * ⏱️ 18 to 22 business days from the date of your final submission

During this process:
                                 * Your book status will still appear as “In Review” in the dashboard until publication is complete

                                 * The internal steps (file validation, layout, listing, etc.) are prioritized in our queue

📩 Once your book is published, you will:
                                    * Receive the live purchase links by email

                                    * See the updated status as “Published” in your dashboard

We appreciate your patience and assure you that every part of the process is actively being handled by our editorial and production teams.

📌 Why is there a fee on the 21st Century Emily Dickinson Award?
The nominal fee of 4499 INR covers several important aspects of the awards process, including:
Administrative Costs: The fee helps us manage the logistical aspects of the awards, from organizing the nomination process to ensuring the integrity and professionalism of the selection, and then shipping and handling costs.
Certification and Prizes: The fee supports the creation of trophy/certificates and other awards materials that recognize your achievement and can be a valuable addition to your portfolio.
Promotion and Visibility: Part of the fee goes into promoting the awards and the nominees, ensuring your book receives the visibility it deserves among readers and industry professionals
