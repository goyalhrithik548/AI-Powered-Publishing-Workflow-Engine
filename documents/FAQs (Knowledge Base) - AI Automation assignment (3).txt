💰 How Is the Price of My Book Determined?
The final price of your book is set by our market strategy team based on multiple practical factors, including:
* ✅ Total number of pages

* ✅ Cost of paper, printing, and GST

* ✅ Cover printing (color) and lamination

* ✅ Royalty margin for you as the author

This pricing strategy ensures:
   * Your book remains competitively priced and marketable to readers

   * Your royalty share is protected

   * The book reflects its production value without overpricing

💡 We aim for a balance between value, quality, and sales potential.

🔒 How Are My Poems Protected?
We take your intellectual property and data security seriously. Here’s how we protect your submissions:
      * Confidentiality Agreements: Every team member signs a binding agreement to maintain the confidentiality of author content.

      * Secure Submission Systems: All dashboards and backends are encrypted and monitored.

      * Strict Access Control: Only designated publishing team members have access to manuscript content.

      * You Retain Full Ownership: We claim no rights over your poems. You are the sole copyright holder.

      * Security Audits: Our system is reviewed periodically for vulnerabilities and compliance.

✅ Your poems will never be shared, published elsewhere, or reused in any form without your explicit written consent.

🎁 Why Did I Receive a Coupon Code Instead of a Physical Author Copy?
Due to the volume of titles we publish and frequent courier issues, physical author copy deliveries have been challenging to manage at scale.
To ensure:
               * A faster and more reliable delivery experience

               * A better tracking experience for you

...we’ve shifted to issuing coupon codes redeemable at the BookLeaf Store.
You can order your book at your preferred time and address using this code — cutting down delays and increasing flexibility.
📌 For authors who chose Bestseller Breakthrough Package, the coupon code will not be sent, instead we will directly ship 5 copies to their address (applicable in India).

💯 What Does “100% Royalty” Mean in the Bestseller Breakthrough Package?
When we say “100% royalty”, it means:
You receive 100% of the profit from each sale — after all mandatory costs are deducted.
Here’s what is deducted before calculating your royalty:
                           * Printing and paper costs

                           * Platform commissions (e.g., Amazon's cut)

                           * Packaging and handling

                           * GST and transaction fees

                           * Discount structures and return buffers

📘 Example:
If your book is listed at ₹150:
                              * Total deductions might be ₹80 (example)

                              * Your royalty will be calculated on the ₹70 profit, not the ₹150 price

✅ This ensures you receive the entire profit, unlike standard publishing models where only a small fraction is shared.
To see how much you’ll earn per copy, use the calculator:
🔗 https://www.bookleafpub.in/printing-cost-royalty-calculator

📚 Can I Get a Draft Copy to Upload on KDP or Another Platform?
No, we do not provide draft files or source files for upload on Kindle Direct Publishing (KDP) or other platforms using our ISBN.
If you'd like to publish your book elsewhere, you are welcome to do so using:
                                    * A different ISBN

                                    * A version that does not use our logo or branding

📌 Also note:
                                       * The eBook version of your book is exclusive to BookLeaf’s bookstore
                                       * Dual listing on Amazon via KDP using our ISBN is not allowed, to avoid publishing conflicts

❗ Why Is My Book Showing “Out of Stock” or “Unavailable” on Amazon?
This issue may appear occasionally due to Amazon’s internal system syncing delays. This is a common occurrence.
Here’s what you should know:
                                          * Your book is still live and published in our system

                                          * Amazon may temporarily show “Currently Unavailable” or “Out of Stock”

                                          * This typically resolves within 24–48 hours

✅ What you should do:
                                             * Check the listing again after a few hours

                                             * If the issue persists beyond 48 hours, raise a support ticket so we can escalate it

________________




🟢 Why Isn’t My Book Marked as “Prime” on Amazon Yet?
As soon as your book is listed on Amazon.in, we submit a request for it to be made Prime eligible.
However:
                                                * The decision and timeline is entirely controlled by Amazon

                                                * Approval may take time due to their internal review process

We are actively monitoring your listing and will follow up as needed. Thank you for your patience while Amazon finalizes the Prime eligibility.
________________




🚚 I Have Amazon Prime — Why Am I Still Being Charged Delivery?
Even if you're a Prime member, Amazon sometimes applies shipping fees on:
                                                   * Print-on-demand products

                                                   * Books fulfilled by third-party sellers

Since BookLeaf books are POD (print-on-demand), Amazon handles the shipping fee directly.
📌 We do not control Amazon’s delivery charges and cannot remove them, even for Prime users.
